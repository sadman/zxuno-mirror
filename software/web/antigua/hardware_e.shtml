Specifications (not final):
  • FPGA Xilinx Spartan XC3S250E-4TQG144C
  • Static Memory 512Kb, CY62148EV30
  • 50MHz Oscillator
  • EAR Superfo Circuit (one transistor)
  • Circuit AD724 based video with 4.43MHz crystal
  • PS/2 keyboard
  • RCA connector for composite video
  • Stereo audio jack
  • EAR jack connector
  • 2x6 pin connector for JTAG strip and RGB
  • Slot for SD Cards
  • Expansion port with 3 male pin strips
  • Micro-USB power connector
  • PCB Size: 85x55 mm. (Compatible with Raspberry Pi cases)
